Day 7 Concept: Frequency Counting

This is one of the most important topics in DSA.

Suppose:

arr = [1,2,2,3,3,3,4]

Question:

How many times does each number appear?

Answer:

1 -> 1
2 -> 2
3 -> 3
4 -> 1

This is called frequency counting.

Why Is It Important?

Many interview questions are based on it:

Most frequent element
Duplicate detection
Character counting
Anagrams
Hash Maps
Sliding Window
Using a Dictionary
arr = [1,2,2,3,3,3,4]

freq = {}

for num in arr:
    if num in freq:
        freq[num] += 1
    else:
        freq[num] = 1

print(freq)

Output:

{1:1, 2:2, 3:3, 4:1}
