Linked List – Delete First Node
Observation

We need to remove the first node.

Current list

Head
 ↓

Expected

Head
 ↓
20 → 30 → 40 → None
Draw It

Before

Head
 |
 v
10 → 20 → 30 → 40 → None

After

      10 → 20 → 30 → 40 → None
           ^
           |
         Head

Notice something?

No .next pointer changed.

Only

head

moves.

That is why deletion from the beginning is

O(1)
Pointer(s) Changed

Only

head
Algorithm
Check if the list is empty.
If empty, return.
Otherwise:
head = head.next

Done.
