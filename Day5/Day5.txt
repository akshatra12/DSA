Day 5 Concept: Linear Search

This is your first real searching algorithm.

Suppose:

arr = [10, 20, 30, 40, 50]

Question:

Is 30 present?

How humans search:

Check 10 ❌
Check 20 ❌
Check 30 ✅ Found

This is called Linear Search.

Example
arr = [10,20,30,40,50]

target = 30

for i in arr:
    if i == target:
        print("Found")

Output:

Found
Why This Matters

Every search algorithm starts with this idea.
