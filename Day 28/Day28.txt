📚 Mini Theory

Whenever you see a DSA problem, ask yourself these 7 questions:

Is the array sorted?
Do I need the maximum/minimum?
Am I looking for a pair?
Is there a fixed-size window?
Am I searching for something?
Are duplicates important?
Can I solve it in one traversal?

These questions will help you identify the correct pattern before you write any code.
