Common Interview Mistakes
Mistake 1

Question:

Will this print the last node?

Think.

Mistake 2

Forget

current=current.next

Infinite loop.

Mistake 3

Changing

head=head.next

instead of

current=current.next

If you move head, you'll lose the beginning of the list.
