Day 8 Concept: Strings

A string is simply a sequence of characters.

Example:

name = "akshatra"

Think of it like an array:

Index	Character
0	a
1	k
2	s
3	h
4	a
5	t
6	r
7	a
Traversing a String
word = "hello"

for ch in word:
    print(ch)

Output:

h
e
l
l
o
String Reversal
word = "hello"

print(word[::-1])

Output:

olleh
Palindrome

A palindrome reads the same forwards and backwards.

Examples:

madam
racecar
level

Logic:

word = "madam"

if word == word[::-1]:
    print("Palindrome")
else:
    print("Not Palindrome")
